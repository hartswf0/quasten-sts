Focused Problem Statement:

Scientific knowledge practices, particularly interpretations of quantum physics, often rely on representationalist frameworks (Level 3 Framing) that assume a pre-existing separation between observer and observed, words and world (Level 4 Logic). This obscures the fundamentally entangled nature of reality (Level 0) where measurement apparatuses (Level 1 Machine) and phenomena are co-constituted through "intra-actions." By modeling measurement as accessing rather than enacting reality, this framework hides the ontological agency of apparatuses and the ethical responsibilities inherent in the "agential cuts" that produce specific phenomena (Level 2 Interpretation/Use).

1. Core Model and Index

Barad, K. (2007). Meeting the universe halfway. Duke University Press.

MODEL: Argues for "agential realism," proposing that matter and meaning are not separate but co-constituted through "intra-actions" within phenomena; agency is not a human attribute but emerges from these material-discursive entanglements, challenging representationalism and emphasizing the material world's active role in knowledge production.

Index:

◻ Agential Realism (framework)

◻ Phenomena → (constituted by) → ◻ Intra-actions (entanglement of matter-meaning)

◻ Apparatuses (material-discursive practices) → (enact) → ◻ Cuts / Distinctions

◻ Agency → (emerges from) → ◻ Intra-actions

◻ Matter ↔ ◻ Meaning (inseparable)

SPATIOTEMPORAL FRAMEWORK

◻ SPATIOTEMPORAL UNIVERSE: Onto-epistemological reality; the entanglement of matter and meaning.

→ [Contains] → ◻ FIELD: Material-discursive practices and phenomena.

→ [Has] → ◻ DOMAIN: Spacetimemattering (dynamic, relational configurations).

→ [Projects] → ◻ SIGNAL: Observable effects, diffractive patterns, measurement outcomes, emergent properties resulting from intra-actions. (ℝᵏ representing measured values, relational configurations).

→ [Admits] → ◻ PERTURBATION: Any "intra-action" (measurement, observation, entanglement) which reconfigures the phenomenon/apparatus relation.

→ [Observed Through] → ◻ WINDOW: Agential Realism (drawing on Bohr, feminist theory, post-structuralism).

→ [Defines] → ◻ OBSERVATION SCALE: Ontological – the level of intra-action where distinctions are enacted.

→ [Delimits] → ◻ CONTEXT BOUNDARY: Rejects pre-existing subject/object divide; the boundary ("agential cut") is part of the phenomenon.

→ [Generates] → ◻ SAMPLING LATTICE: Specific experimental apparatuses, ethical situations, quantum phenomena interpreted as sites of intra-action.

→ [Manifests As] → ◻ PHENOMENON: The co-constitution of knowing and being through intra-actions; agency as an emergent relational property, not an attribute; the "agential cut."

◻ ENGINE: Agential Realist Analysis.

→ [Applies] → ◻ TRANSFORM: Shifting analysis from interaction between entities to intra-action within phenomena; Diffraction over reflection.

→ [Operates On] → ◻ PATCH: A specific phenomenon-apparatus configuration (e.g., two-slit experiment, fetal ultrasound).

→ [Produces] → ◻ INVARIANT: The agential cut; the specific material-discursive entanglement enacted; responsibility within entanglement.

→ [Requires] → ◻ CONTRACTION: Focusing on the constitutive nature of boundaries and distinctions enacted within the phenomenon.

→ [Constructs] → ◻ REPRESENTATION: A model of reality as dynamic, relational becoming; matter and meaning inseparable; ethics inherent in ontology.

→ [Optimizes] → ◻ RECURSION: The way phenomena and apparatuses recursively define and enact each other through ongoing intra-actions.

→ [Embeds] → ◻ MODEL: Quantum field theory (Bohr's interpretation), post-structuralist theories of discourse/power, feminist epistemology.

→ [Projects] → ◻ METRIC: Ethical accountability ("response-ability") arising from entanglement and enacted cuts (qualitative).

→ [Seeks] → ◻ FIXED POINT: Understanding the specific material-discursive configuration enacted by an agential cut (always provisional, dynamic).
