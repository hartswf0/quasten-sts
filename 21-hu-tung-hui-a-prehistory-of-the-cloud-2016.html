Hu Tung Hui A Prehistory Of The Cloud 2016

1. Problem Statement

  1. Hu, Tung-Hui - A Prehistory of the Cloud (2016)

Focused Problem Statement: Accepting the metaphor of the "Cloud" (L1 Interface simulation) as implying immaterial, ubiquitous storage (L4 Ideology) obscures the vast, physical infrastructure (data centers, cables - L0 Reality) it relies upon. Hu's media archaeology (L3 Framing) reveals this material "prehistory," modeling Cloud logic as inheriting spatial and control strategies from Cold War systems, challenging the user's (L2) dematerialized perception.

2. Core Model

21. Hu, Tung-Hui - A Prehistory of the Cloud (2016)

  • MODEL: Argues that the seemingly ethereal and decentralized "Cloud" has a material and ideological "prehistory" in earlier logistical, infrastructural, and governmental systems (like Cold War bunkers, data centers, fiber optic networks), revealing its dependence on physical infrastructure and inherited logics of control and security.
  • Index:
    • ◻ The Cloud (Contemporary Concept: Ethereal/Decentralized)
    • ◻ Earlier Logistical/Infrastructural/Governmental Systems → ◻ Prehistory of the Cloud
    • ◻ Prehistory → ◻ Reveals Cloud's Materiality / Physical Infrastructure
    • ◻ Prehistory → ◻ Reveals Cloud's Inherited Logics (Control/Security)

      • → [Delimits] → ◻ CONTEXT BOUNDARY: Connects contemporary Cloud infrastructure to earlier systems of control, logistics, and militarization; challenges dematerialized views.

    • → [Applies] → ◻ TRANSFORM: Unearthing the historical layers and material substrates beneath the Cloud metaphor, revealing continuities in logic and form.
