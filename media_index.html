MEDIA Analysis Index

Primarily contemporary society, characterized by pervasive technological mediation, but dynamically extending into historical contexts (from pre-history of media/computation to specific genealogies of power, aesthetics, and technology). The Universe evolves from industrial/mass media contexts (Adorno, Benjamin) towards late capitalist/postmodern (Jameson, Debord) and increasingly networked, digital, datafied realities (Brock, Chun, Hayles, Turkle, Manovich, Jenkins).
